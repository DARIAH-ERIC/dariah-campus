Do you want to create a digital scholarly edition? Then this course is for you!
Here you will learn to master the technologies of XML, the Text Encoding
Initiative (TEI), and XSLT to produce great digital editions. You will learn to
create critical editions, facsimile editions and useful visualizations of your
editions.
