Polytechnic Institute, USA. In this video she discusses Mixed Reality and Social
Engagement. Tamar talks about Augmented Reality as a tool that can make history
come alive, while helping us to interpret cultural-historical environments and
reflect upon our own experience and subject position within our own society.
