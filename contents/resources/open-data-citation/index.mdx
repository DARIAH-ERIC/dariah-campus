The DARIAH Winter School "Open Data Citation for Social Sciences and Humanities"
brought together researchers, professionals with various backgrounds, and
students from 15 countries. In total 38 people met in Prague, Czech Republic, to
learn about various aspects of open access and open data, as well as many other
subjects on digital research.

The Winter School was organized by DARIAH, as part of its Humanities at Scale
project, and hosted by Prague's Charles University. HaS-DARIAH has received
funding from the EU Horizon 2020 Research and Innovation Programme under Grant
Agreement No. 675570.
