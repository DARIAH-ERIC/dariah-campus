This workshop introduces participants to the background, and certain
technologies and methods used for the spatial analysis of image datasets. The
introduction provides a broad overview of the value of exploring the spatial
properties of images while the rest of the workshop focuses on the methods of
geotagging and spatial analysis through concrete examples and hands-on
exercises.
