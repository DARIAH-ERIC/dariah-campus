This course invites you to discover the world of digital multimodal literacies
through examples, experiments, readings and tools. Unit I consists of an
introduction to multimodal literacies. Unit II allows you to discover, read and
experiment with different examples of multimodal literacies. Finally, Unit III
will allow you to build your own multimodal editing tool, an eTalk, on a virtual
machine. Enjoy your travels in multimodal literacies!
