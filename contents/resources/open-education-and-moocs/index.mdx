This video features Prof. Graeme Earl (Director of Enterprise and Impact
(Humanities), University of Southampton) talking about the benefits and
challenges of Open Education and Massive Open Online Courses. This video was
recorded during the Virtual Heritage Network Conference (December 2016, Cork,
Ireland) while he was Professor of Digital Humanities at the University of
Southampton. Prof. Earl subsequently moved to King's College London where he is
a Professor of Digital Humanities.
