This course introduces you to the definition of games, core concepts and
theories from game studies as well as the cultural importance of games and the
critical potentials and possibilities for change that games hold.

You will also learn about the process of designing games (also for newcomers and
non-designers), the game jam format and the potentials and benefits of doing
game jams. You will get to make a game concept in 10 minutes as well as try out
the game jam format.
