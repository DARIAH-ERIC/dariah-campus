Lukas Plank studied Philology in Vienna and is part of the research unit DH Research&Infrastructure. He is currently working on the Computational Literary Studies Infrastructure project and the Releven project at the University of Vienna as a Python developer focussing on semantic technologies.

Lukas' academic interests include computational philology, editorial science and graph-based knowledge representation.
