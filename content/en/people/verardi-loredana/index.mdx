Software engineer, PhD  and co-founder & CTO of SmartEducationLab, a technological SME spin-off of CETMA (European Research Centre for Technology, Design and Materials). Her work focuses on IoT, AI, and Digital Twin solutions in the domains of Smart Agriculture and Cultural Heritage. She is a certified Innovation Manager (UNI 11814) and member of the Italian task force for the National Recovery and Resilience Plan (NRRP) on the digitalization of complex procedures. She has extensive experience in R\&D and software development. She has been a lecturer in post-graduate programs on Information Systems and Databases, and has delivered courses on process modeling and simulation, IoT, and educational robotics.
