Marlene Albrecht studied Romance languages with a focus on linguistics at the University of Vienna. During her master’s degree in Digital Humanities, she specialised in Natural Language Processing and Computational Linguistics. Before joining the ACDH, she worked as an instructor for German as a second and foreign language, as well as literacy. At the ACDH, she works as a Training Officer in the research unit DH Research & Infrastructure and coordinates the internship programme. She also participates in the ARTEMIS project (Applying Reactive Twins to Enhance Monument Information Systems).
