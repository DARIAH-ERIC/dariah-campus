Lucía Iglesias is a Project Coordinator in the Spanish SME HolaVR working to bridge the gap between academic theory and immersive technology. Drawing on an MA in Anthropology and Film & Visual Culture, she specializes in project development for innovative VR tools within heritage sites and culturally significant contexts. Her main focus is creating digital resources that respect and elevate diverse narratives, ensuring that cutting-edge technology makes heritage accessible, engaging and educationally impactful.
