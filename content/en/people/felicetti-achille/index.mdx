Achille Felicetti is a researcher at VAST-LAB PIN, University of Florence, working since 2004 on digital technologies for cultural heritage. His work focuses on metadata, standards, ontologies, semantic tools, and AI applications for research, conservation, and enhancement. He has contributed to major European projects such as ARIADNE, PARTHENOS, EOSC, and 4CH, and is a member of ARIADNE RI AISBL as well as initiatives like ATRIUM, ARTEMIS and ECHOES. He has coordinated ontology development, interoperable research platforms, and machine learning tools. He is a member of the CIDOC CRM SIG, coordinating the CRMarchaeo and CRMtex extensions.
