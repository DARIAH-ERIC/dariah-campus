Beatrix Howe is Project Manager at NUMENA, a research-driven technology company operating at the crossroads of spatial design, human-computer interaction, and future studies. Her background is in architecture and urbanism, combined with graduate research in digital twins and virtual reality. Within the Summer School, she will lead a session on feasibility assessment and validation challenges in complex digital twin and spatial simulation projects.
