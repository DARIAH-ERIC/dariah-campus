**Doran Larson** is Edward North Professor of Literature at Hamilton College. He led a writing workshop inside Attica Correctional Facility for ten years and has organised two college programs inside New York State prisons. The author of *Witness in the Era of Mass Incarceration* (2017), and editor of Fourth City: Essays from the Prison in America (2014), his book *Inside Knowledge: Incarcerated People on the Failures of the American Prison*, was published in 2024. He founded and co-directs the American Prison Writing Archive.
