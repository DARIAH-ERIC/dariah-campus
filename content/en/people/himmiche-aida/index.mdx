Aida Himmiche is a specialist in Artificial Intelligence and Data Engineering at ARIDANE Research Infrastructure. Her work focuses on designing and implementing AI-driven solutions for projects in the cultural heritage domain. In the ARTEMIS project, she develops AI models to enhance and exploit a graph-based semantic knowledge base, along with intelligent user interface features that enable intuitive information retrieval. Her contributions aim to improve access to complex cultural data and support its use in Heritage Digital Twin applications.
