Dr James Smithies is Director of King's Digital Lab. He was previously Senior Lecturer in Digital Humanities and Associate Director of the UC CEISMIC Digital Archive at the University of Canterbury, New Zealand. He has worked in the government and commercial IT sectors as a technical writer and editor, business analyst, and project manager. His monograph _The Digital Humanities and the Digital Modern_ was published by Palgrave Macmillan in 2017.
