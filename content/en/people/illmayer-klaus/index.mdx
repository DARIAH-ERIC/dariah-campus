Klaus Illmayer is Research Software Engineer at Austrian Centre of Digital Humanities and Cultural Heritage (ACDH-CH) at Austrian Academy of Sciences. He holds a PhD in Theatre, Film, and Media Studies from University of Vienna. His research interests focus on digital documenting and archiving of cultural expressions – especially theatrical events – as well as data modelling and establishing digital workflows for research.
At ACDH-CH he was involved in digital infrastructure projects like PARTHENOS and SSHOC and is currently working in EOSC Future.
