Maria Laura Leonardi is a PhD candidate in Civil Engineering at the University of Minho, where she focuses on the seismic assessment of unreinforced masonry buildings. Her research is dedicated to enhancing the evaluation and preservation of historic structures by integrating Building Information Modeling (BIM) into structural analysis workflows.
