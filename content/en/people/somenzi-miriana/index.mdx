Miriana Somenzi is a researcher in Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing at ARIADNE Research Infrastructure. Within the ARTEMIS projects, she works with AI models, such as LLMs, and Knowledge Graphs to develop efficient and intelligent solutions that support not only the understanding and management of unstructured cultural data, but also reasoning and predictive capabilities, contributing to the advancement of Heritage Digital Twins.
