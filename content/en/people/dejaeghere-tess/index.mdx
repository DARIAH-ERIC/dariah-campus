Tess Dejaeghere has a background in Translation and Interpretation (VUB), Digital Humanities (KULeuven) and Digital Text Analysis (University of Antwerp).  She is currently working on a PhD in Digital Humanities at the University of Ghent (Center for Digital Humanities) under the supervision of Dr. Julie Birkholz, with a focus on the development of NLP workflows and toolchains for digital and non-digital humanists.
