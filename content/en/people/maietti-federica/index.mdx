Federica Maietti, M.Arch., PhD, is Associate Professor at the Department of Architecture of the University of Ferrara. Her interdisciplinary research activities are focused on heritage survey and representation for diagnosis, conservation, restoration, and enhancement through digital technologies. Since 2020, she has been a founding member of the spin-off Inception.
