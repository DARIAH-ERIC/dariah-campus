Vicky Dritsou is an informatician specialized in semantic databases, conceptual modelling, and knowledge representation. She is a Senior Research Associate at the Digital Curation Unit of the Athena Research Center and a Research Fellow at the Athens University of Economics and Business (AUEB). She has contributed to numerous national and European research projects as an expert in semantic web technologies and knowledge organization, including work as the scientific lead for her institute. She teaches topics related to conceptual modelling, knowledge organization, and the curation and publication of digital assets. She holds a BSc in Informatics, an MSc in Information Systems, and a PhD in Informatics, all from the Department of Informatics at AUEB.
