Moritz Mähr is a PhD candidate at Collegium Helveticum Zurich and at the Chair for the History of Technology at ETH Zurich. He examines the emergence of state action in a digital society in Switzerland in the second half of the 20th century in the light of computer history. For this purpose he also builds digital history tools.
