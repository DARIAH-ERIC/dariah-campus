Katrín Lísa L. Mikaelsdóttir holds a Ph.D. in Icelandic Linguistics from the University of Iceland. She currently works as a project manager at the Centre for Digital Humanities and Arts in Reykjavík, as well as the Erasmus+ KA2-funded network ANTIDOTE – Advanced Training in Digitization of Older Texts. Her main research areas include historical linguistics, statistical analysis and data visualization, and advances in Handwritten Text Recognition.
