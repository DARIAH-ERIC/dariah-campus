Caterina F. Carocci, architect and PhD in History of building sciences\
and techniques, has been full professor of Restoration (ICAR/19) since\
2020 at the University of Catania, where she has been teaching in her\
subject area, and has been supervisor of numerous degree theses and\
tutor of some PhD theses. Her research activity is dedicated to the\
topic of conservation of historical masonry architecture.
