Fotis Giariskanis is a multidisciplinary creative technologist and computer engineer specializing in interactive experiences, XR, animation, and serious games. His work spans R\&D, HCI, UX design, and prototyping, focusing on transforming passive audiences into active participants. He has served as a teaching assistant in ECE and as a reviewer for IEEE VR. He is currently working as a Creative Tech Engineer in VR/AR/XR and Research Associate at Athena Research Center, contributing to the Artemis project.
