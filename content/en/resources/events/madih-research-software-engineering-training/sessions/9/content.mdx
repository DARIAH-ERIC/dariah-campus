## Abstract

This final session of the workshop started with the presentation of UK and European DH infrastructures and organisations, a list of links to internationally recognised policies and best practices in DH, important peer-reviewed journals and mailing lists dedicated to diverse aspects of human and technical research infrastructures that may be of relevance to the MaDiH project and of interest to its members.The second part of the session was dedicated to discussion and requirements elicitation for the MaDiH project.
