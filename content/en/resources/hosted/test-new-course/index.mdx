## Learning Outcomes

At the end of this module learners will be able to:

* Understand the fundamental principles of audiovisual grammar.
* Analyze how images and sounds create meaning, narrative effects, and emotional responses.
* Experiment with audiovisual creation.
* Draw on cinematic culture.
* Analyze an audiovisual corpus.
* Annotate video documents.
* Collaborate on video documents to produce research results.
