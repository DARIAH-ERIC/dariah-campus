This course introduces learners to the annotation tool ELAN, developed at the Max Planck Institute for Psycholinguistics. ELAN supports transcribing, annotating and visualising audio and video material. It is designed to create layered or tiered transcriptions. The number of annotations is unlimited and can include anything from translation, comments or feature descriptions. ELAN is a free, open-source tool and runs on various operating systems.
