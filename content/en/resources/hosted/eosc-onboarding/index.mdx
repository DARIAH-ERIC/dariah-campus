This webinar is part of the TRIPLE Open Science Training Series.

The training session is dedicated to the EOSC onboarding and is led by Carsten Thiel, Chief Technical Officer at CESSDA ERIC and Joshua Tetteh Ocansey, Technical Officer at CESSDA ERIC.

This webinar explores the steps needed to get services 'onboarded' and listed on the EOSC marketplace. It specifically gives assistance to service providers in the process of sharing services via EOSC with the EOSC Portal and introduces them to some of the benefits of the EOSC Portal. The minimum criteria to become a provider and the requirements to onboard services into the EOSC Portal are also presented. This training session also incorporates requirements from the TRIPLE project.

You can download the slides here: [https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.5036684](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.5036684)

## Learning Outcomes

After watching this video, you should:

* Understand the minimum criteria to become a service provider for EOSC.
* Understand in detail the EOSC onboarding process.
* Understand the specific requirements to onboard services into the EOSC Portal.
