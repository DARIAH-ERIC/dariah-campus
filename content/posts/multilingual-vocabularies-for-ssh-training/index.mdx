This webinar is part of the [TRIPLE Open Science Training Series](https://project.gotriple.eu/training/).

This training session is a synergy of TRIPLE and SSHOC projects and focuses on controlled vocabularies for Social Sciences and Humanities (SSH). It is led by Daan Broeder with contributions from other SSHOC partners ([CLARIN ERIC](https://www.clarin.eu/)/[SSHOC](https://sshopencloud.eu/)), Haris Georgiadis ([EKT](https://www.ekt.gr/en/index)/[TRIPLE](https://project.gotriple.eu/)) and Nikos Vasilogamvrakis ([EKT](https://www.ekt.gr/en/index)).
