#### Introduction to Advanced Image Generation Techniques in AI

Welcome to an exploration of advanced techniques in AI image generation. In this resource, learners will gain the skills to interface with AI tools in a manner which is efficient and accurate.  You will learn techniques of image generation using AI, primarily focusing on methods that enhance the connection between what you want to see and what the AI tool generates for you. From word weighting to narrative elements, each technique will open new doors to how AI interprets and visualizes our ideas. This resource will equip you with the skills to not just use AI effectively but to know when to employ each technique you learn and why. Honing this skill is a way to blend art, technology, and imagination, transforming the way we perceive and create images in the digital age.

### Learning Outcomes

After completing this training resource, learners should be able to:

- Understand a wide array of prompt engineering techniques useful in text to image AI tools
- Practice discernment in knowing when to use each method and why
- Identify examples of each technique covered in this resource

### Methods

#### 1. Word Weighting

Explanation: This technique involves emphasizing certain keywords in the prompt to steer the AI's focus during image generation.

When to Use: Ideal when specific elements need prominence in the generated image, or when refining the details of an image.

Sample Prompt: "Create an image of a 'sunny, crowded beach' emphasizing 'sunny'."

#### 2. Dataset-Informed Reference Cues

Explanation: Leverages knowledge of the AI's training dataset to create prompts that are more likely to produce accurate and detailed images.

When to Use: Useful when the desired image requires specific details or styles known to be in the AI's training set.

Sample Prompt: "Create an image in the style of a 19th-century landscape painting, as found in your dataset."
