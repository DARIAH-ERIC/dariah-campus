This resource compiles the presentations and event report from the SSHOC / DARIAH Train-the-Trainer Research Data Management Bootcamp, held across 2 half-days in February 2021.

The workshops comprised 4 sessions, each outlining practical approaches for course providers when teaching research data management:

**Session 1** - "Planning to meet the costs of managing research data to be FAIR"\
**Instructors:** Angus Whyte, Annalisa Montesanti, Joy Davidson, Ryan O'Connor

Session 2 - "GDPR and Ethical issues in working with social media data"\
Instructors: Katrin Weller, Oliver Watteler
