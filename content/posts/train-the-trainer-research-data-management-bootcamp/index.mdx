This resource compiles the presentations and event report from the SSHOC / DARIAH Research Data Management Working Group "Train-the-Trainer Research Data Management Bootcamp", held across 2 half-days in February 2021.

The workshop comprised 4 sessions, each outlining practical approaches for course providers when teaching research data management:

- **Session 1 - "Planning to meet the costs of managing research data to be FAIR"**\
  **Instructors:** Angus Whyte, Annalisa Montesanti, Joy Davidson, Ryan O'Connor
- **Session 2 - "GDPR and Ethical issues in working with social media data"**\
  **Instructors:** Katrin Weller, Oliver Watteler
- **Session 3 - "Dealing with third-party data coming from Cultural Heritage institutions (galleries, museums, libraries, archives)"**\
  **Instructor:** Kristen Schuster
- **Session 4 - "Research Data Management Didactics"**\
  **Instructor:** Kerstin Helbig

Presentation slides and a video of different sessions are available.

The 'Train-the-Trainers Research Data Management Bootcamp' was co-organised by the Social Sciences and Humanities Open Cloud ([SSHOC](https://sshopencloud.eu/)) project, and the [DARIAH Research Data Management Working Group](https://www.dariah.eu/activities/working-groups/research-data-management/).
