Dr. Kristen Schuster presents her ongoing work with Dr Vanessa Reyes around unwaged labour and gender biases in the Information Management sector during the Covid-19 Pandemic. In this, she discusses Critical Feminist Theory, Personal Information Spaces (PSIs) and mixed method approaches to research.

This webinar took place as part of the DARIAH Friday Frontiers In-House Webinar series.

You can download Kristen's slides here: [King's College London Sharepoint](https://emckclac-my.sharepoint.com/:p:/g/personal/k1517875_kcl_ac_uk/EY-QHoalhfxOiUAc-M9CsvcBKGhA_Kfff49j_1Im9O7W6g?e=9js4xa)

## Learning Outcomes

After watching this video, you should:

- understand what Personal Information Spaces (PSIs) are
- understand issues that have arisen within archival practices due to the Covid-19 Pandemic
- understand how mixed-methods research approaches work in Digital Humanities research
