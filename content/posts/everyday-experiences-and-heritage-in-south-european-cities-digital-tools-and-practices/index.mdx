abstract: >-
  On Monday, July 26th, the Order of Architects of Palermo participated in the
  DARIAH Workshop on applications developed by DARIAH Workgroup in cooperation
  with university research partners. 

  In particular, DARIAH presented and discussed the app WIKAR, developed by Colter Wehmeier from the University of Illinois. WIKAR uses AR technologies to facilitate participatory project development processes. The workshop participants could visualize in a digital environment various project proposals directly projected in the real spaces subject of the project through IOS/Android mobile devices.
